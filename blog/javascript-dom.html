JavaScript and the DOM

An explanation of what I've learned about the DOM, JavaScript, and some of their features

Explaining HTML, CSS, and JavaScript

HTML forms the base and structure of a website, and where you put the content of a website. Imagine it like a blank birthday card, with: “Dear dad. Happy Birthday!”


CSS is how the content on your page is styled. Imagine it like choosing the color and font of the birthday card.


JavaScript can make a website interactive, and change content under conditions you choose. Think of it like the birthday card playing music when you open it, and stopping when you close it.
