Neuroplasticity and Growth Mindset

A discussion on Neuroplasticity and Growth Mindset, and how it relates to my learning journey

Neuroplasticity

Neuroplasticity is the ability of the brain and nervous system to change in response to experience. Neuroplasticity takes several different forms, and can happen in response to many things. It can happen from stress or trauma, from learning, or even from injury. May times, neuroplasticity happens without your concious intention. But amazingly, we can make it happen through concious action.


The incredable, also possibly surprising thing, is that we can purposly self-induce neuroplasticity. There is research to incicate that we can support neuroplasticity from:

Learning (eg. a new skill or language)

Getting enough sleep

Mindfulness

Creative pursuits


Understanding that we can have direct input into how our brain functions is incredibly powerful. For me, knowing that I'm not completely at the mercy of how my brain is hardwired, gives me more agency and peace with myself. It also helps me to be conscious what activities will help me and why, and provides more motivation to do those things that will benefit my neuroplasticity.
