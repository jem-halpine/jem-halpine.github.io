Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence

Emotional Intelligence, or Emotional Quotient (EQ) is one's ability to recognise, evaluate, and manage their emotions. EQ also concerns one's ability to recognise and understand other's emotions.

Why Emotional Intelligence is useful

The impact of EQ can be seen every day of your life. Having a strong EQ is arguably essential for long-term wellbeing. Improving EQ, while empowering you with the ability to regulate your emotions, will also help you to make better decisions, with more clarity and agency. Of course, everything social or interpersonal is effected by EQ, helping you to foster productive, healthy, and fulfilling relationships.

Strengthening Emotional Intelligence

Some ways to improve EQ include:

Practicing mindfulness Mindfulness involves focusing fully on the present moment. For many, consistent mindfulness practice helps with emotional awareness and regulation.

Self reflection Spending time observing and reflecting on your thoughts and feels can bring greater self awareness. Journaling is a great way to do this.

Practicing Empathy Practice imagining yourself in someone else's position. Make an effort to consider any different feelings or perspectives they might have.
