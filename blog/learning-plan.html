Learning Plan


My goals and commitments to learning at Dev Academy

Long term goals

My longterm goal is to become an adept developer, and to become familiar with other sectors in tech. I feel there's a lot of satisfaction to being a talented developer, but I'm also interested to see if my people skills are useful in adjacent areas, like tech sales or recruitment, or even project management. Overall, I'm aiming to develop well-rounded, and see where I can find the most joy and satisfaction.

Strengths and limitations for learning

I am at my strongest learning when others are relying on me to learn and perform. If there's a group project I need to be ready for, you bet I'll nail it. I'm the weakest I'm learning and working on my own volition, I struggle to find motivation and commit to tasks.

Skills to develop

n my time at Dev Academy, I'm hoping to build confidence and ability working by myself. I'm excited to develop tools and strategies to work effectively and efficiently, and reaffirm my self belief.

My commitment to managing workloads

I commit to prioritizing my learning and coursework first and foremost. I will communicate often with other learners and facilitators, to build the enthusiasm to learn, and then give that enthusiasm back to others. When the workload becomes too much, I'll be honestly and forthcoming, look for any advice or criticism, and listen to any criticisms seriously

My commitment to seeking help

While remote during foundations, I'll reach out to our lovely community via discord. I'm better at explaining an issue in person, so during bootcamp, I'll chat to other learners and facilitators when I need help.

Non course commitments

I am all but free of big responsibilities, my top commitment now is my learning. My only obligation is to my tennis team every other Sunday.
